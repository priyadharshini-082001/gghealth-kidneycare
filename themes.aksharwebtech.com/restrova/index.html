Chapter One - The Beginning

Born from
One Obsession

Restrova began not with a business plan, but with a question: what if a restaurant refused to compromise on a single thing? No frozen stock. No pre-packaged sauces. Just fire, skill, and the very best ingredients we could find.

  • Uncompromising Quality

    We accept only the finest — 28-day aged beef, wild-caught fish, single-origin truffles. Quality is not a policy; it is an obsession.

  • Radical Freshness

    Everything made fresh daily. Every stock, every sauce, every pasta. Nothing frozen, nothing pre-packaged. Ever.

  • Community First

    We source from local farmers, train local talent, and grow with the community that gave us our start.

Our History

Four Decades of Flavour

From a humble family kitchen tucked in the old quarter to a celebrated dining institution
every dish we craft carries the weight of tradition, the warmth of love, and the fire of ambition.

The Beginning
Old Quarter, Downtown

A Family Dream Takes Root

Antonio and Maria Rossi opened a tiny 12-table trattoria, arming it only with three-generation family recipes and a wood-fired oven. The aroma alone drew queues around the block from opening week.

Contact Support
01

You can book a table directly on our website using the Reserve a Table button at the top of any page. Simply choose your preferred date, time, party size and occasion — we will confirm your booking instantly by email. For larger groups of 8 or more, we recommend calling us directly on +00 (99) 939 7777.

Weekend tables at 7pm and 8pm fill up fast. We recommend booking at least 3 days in advance for Friday and Saturday evenings.

Book a table now
02

Yes — we deliver directly from the kitchen, 7 days a week between 12pm and 10pm. We cover a 10-mile radius from our restaurant. All orders are packed in insulated, temperature-controlled boxes so your food arrives exactly as it left our kitchen. Delivery is free on orders over $60.

Average delivery time is 35–45 minutes. You will receive a live tracking link by SMS once your order is on its way.

03

Every Restrova meal kit includes all the pre-measured, restaurant-quality ingredients you need to recreate the dish at home. The kit also includes a printed recipe card and a QR code linking to a personal video walkthrough by the chef who created the dish. Nothing is frozen — everything is dispatched fresh on the morning of delivery.

Browse all meal kits
04

Absolutely. We source 100% of our produce within 150 miles of the restaurant. Every farmer, fisherman and artisan supplier is personally known to us — we visit them regularly and they are named in our menus. We never use frozen ingredients, pre-packaged sauces, or anything we would not be proud to tell you about.

Our Wagyu beef comes exclusively from a single farm in the Cotswolds, and our truffles are sourced from Périgord, France — both within our supply philosophy of radical transparency.

05

Yes. Please include your dietary requirements in the Special Requests field when booking or ordering. We accommodate gluten-free, dairy-free, vegetarian and vegan diets. For severe allergies, please call us directly so we can speak with the kitchen team to ensure your meal is prepared safely and without cross-contamination.

06

Table reservations can be cancelled free of charge up to 24 hours before your booking time. For shop orders and meal kits, you can cancel and receive a full refund within 12 hours of placing your order, provided the kit has not yet been dispatched. Once dispatched, we are unable to offer refunds on perishable items.

If anything arrives damaged or below our standards, we will always make it right — no questions asked. Simply contact us within 24 hours of delivery.

For private dining and events, we offer a dedicated sommelier service. Our meal kits also include a wine pairing recommendation card in every box.
